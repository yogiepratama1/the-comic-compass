## Historical Context: Why Crisis Happened

### The Problem: Too Many Earths

DC Comics punya problem: setiap Elseworlds story, alternate universe, menciptakan new Earth. Result:
- Readers confused tentang "canonical" universe
- Multiple versions same characters
- Writers nggak tahu what's in continuity
- **It was a mess.**

### The Solution: Crisis Event

Marv Wolfman & George PÃ©rez created Crisis to:
- **Consolidate multiverse** into single universe
- **Reboot continuity** dari scratch
- **Permanently change** iconic heroes
- **Make consequences matter**

## Why Crisis Matters So Much

### 1. **It Changed Comics Forever**
Crisis set template for modern events:
- âœ… Multiverse-spanning action
- âœ… Permanent consequences
- âœ… Character transformations
- âœ… Continuity resets

Every big crossover since copies Crisis structure.

### 2. **Character Deaths MEANT Something**
Unlike modern comics where nobody stays dead, Crisis deaths stuck:
- Supergirl: Dead until 2015
- Barry Allen Flash: Dead until 2008
- **20+ year absences.** That's HUGE.

### 3. **It Unified DC Universe**
Post-Crisis, DC had cleaner continuity for ~20 years. Made comic world less confusing.

### 4. **Shaped Superman & Batman Forever**
Post-Crisis Superman & Batman continuity lasted until 2011. That's 25 YEARS influenced by Crisis changes.
